% of Total Population Fully Vaccinated and Partially Vaccinated

Although Malaysia's vaccination rollout started out slow, it picked up quite fast. As of 31st August 2021, 47.4% of Malaysia's population has been fully vaccinated while 62.1% are partially vaccinated. According to Malaysia's National Security Council, an individual is fully vaccinated against Covid-19 14 days after receiving the second dose of Pfizer, Astrazeneca or Sinovac vaccines; or 28 days after a single dose of Johnson & Johnson or Cansino vaccines.

Malaysia's Vaccine Portfolio

As part of the COVID-19 Vaccines Global Access (COVAX) initiative, Malaysia, as a self-financing participant, has purchased 6.6 million doses of vaccines manufactured by Oxford-AstraZeneca. Altogether, Malaysia has purchased 66.7 million doses of Covid-19 vaccines covering 109.65% of the population. As of 31 August 2021, Malaysia's National Immunisation Program (PICK) has been exclusively for those above 18 years old. According to coordinating minister, Khairy Jamaluddin, those below 18 years old will only be inoculated after the adult population has been fully vaccinated in September.
