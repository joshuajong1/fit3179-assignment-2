A look into the Covid-19 Pandemic in Malaysia

A visualisation by Joshua Jong







Percentage of the Population are Fully Vaccinated and Partially Vaccinated

Malaysia has purchased 66.7 million doses of Covid-19 vaccines covering 109.65% of the population.
As part of the COVID-19 Vaccines Global Access (COVAX) initiative, Malaysia, as a self-financing participant, has purchased 6.6 million doses of vaccines manufactured by Oxford-AstraZeneca.
